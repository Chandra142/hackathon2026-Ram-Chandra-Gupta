# Customer Support Knowledge Base

## Return Policy
We offer a **30-day return window** for all purchases. 
- Items must be in their original condition.
- Refunds are only processed for orders marked as **Delivered**.
- Orders still in 'Processing' or 'Shipped' status are not yet eligible for a refund.

## Exchange & Replacement Policy
- If an item arrives **damaged or defective**, we provide a full refund or a replacement.
- Replacement requests currently require review by a human agent to ensure stock availability.

## Shipping & Delivery
- **Processing Time**: 1-2 business days.
- **Shipping Time**: Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days.
- **Tracking**: Tracking information is updated within 24 hours of shipment.

## Cancellation Policy
- Orders can be cancelled if they are still in the **Processing** stage.
- Once an order has shifted to **Shipped**, it can no longer be cancelled; however, it can be returned once delivered.
